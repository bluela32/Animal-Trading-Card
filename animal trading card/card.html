Welsh Corgi

The Queen owned more than 30 corgis. Corgi means "dwarf dog".

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus Familiaris
  • Weight: Between 22 and 30 pounds
  • Height: 9.84 inch - 12 inch
  • Types: Pembroke Welsh and Cardigan Welsh

Corgis were originally bred to become herding dogs. They are loyal and intelligent.
